Intended Audience

My audience for my weather site is those that live in the cities that I am displaying the weather for. My audience is also my WDD230 Instructor.

2 Personas:

Persona 1: Matt

  • Lives in Preston, ID
  • College student
  • Lives on coffee
  • In a long distance relationship

Persona 2: Lacey

  • Lives in Soda Springs
  • Senior in HS
  • Hobbies: Fashion & Music
  • Goes to bear lake on the weekends

5 Scenarios:

  1. Matt is usually pretty busy with his college classes. However, this weekend he is going up to Soda Springs. He's going to visit his cousin, Lacey. He checks the weather to make sure he doesn't need to bring a jacket.
  2. Lacey frequently likes to dress according to the weather. After all, she has a prefect outfit for every occasion and temperature. She checks the weather with Rain, Cloud, Shine to make sure she picks the perfect outfit.
  3. Lacey loves to go to bear lake with her friends. This week she'll be staying in Fish Haven. Before packing, Lacey checks what the weather is going to be in Fish Haven.
  4. Matt lives in Preston, Idaho. He finds himself occasionally checking the weather at the beginning of the week. He usually likes to look at the weather for the whole week.
  5. Ben wants to suprise his family with a vacation to bear lake. Before getting work off and telling his family, Ben checks the weather in Fish Haven to make sure there isn't bad weather.
